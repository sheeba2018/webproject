Research

You are to research a web technology of your choice. You will describe what it is, and its advantages and disadvantages. You must write at least 600 words of meaningful content NO FLUFF, (cited sources do not count as words). This must be in your own words, you must have at least one source and you must cite your source(s). Your cited sources will be in a ordered list, in which the publication or web page will be in a cite element, see example. Also your last item in your sources list will be the number of how many words your research project is.

Some suggested topics are. (NOTE: These are just an example you can pick any web topic or web technology)

  • AJAX (Asynchronous JavaScript and XML)
  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • CGI (Common Gateway Interface)
  • XSL (Extensible Stylesheet Language)

Example of doing your source citing (view source code to see the structure)

  1. Robert Redfield "The Folk Society." American Journal of Sociology, LII (1947) pp. 293-308
  2. Wikipedia - Ajax (no author listed) https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ajax_(programming)
  3. Word Count (number or words in your research topic)
